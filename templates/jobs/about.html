About Berhan Bank

Ethiopia's trusted financial partner, empowering growth and prosperity through innovative banking solutions and career opportunities.

Our Story

Berhan Bank S.C. was established by 11,200 shareholders with a paid-up capital of Birr 308 million and commenced operations on October 1, 2009. The Bank was officially registered with the National Bank of Ethiopia on July 27, 2009, under the Licensing & Supervision of Banking Business Proclamation No. 592/2008 and the Commercial Code of Ethiopia of 1960. Since then, we have grown to become one of Ethiopia's leading private banks, serving customers through our extensive network of branches nationwide.

Our History

From our founding in 2009, we've consistently grown to become a trusted leader in Ethiopian banking, expanding our services and branch network year after year.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative financial solutions that drive Ethiopia's economic growth while creating value for our stakeholders and communities.

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, innovation, customer focus, and social responsibility form the foundation of our operations.
