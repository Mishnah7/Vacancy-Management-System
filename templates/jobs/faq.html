Application Process

To apply for a position at Berhan Bank:
  1. Browse available positions on our career portal
  2. Create an account or log in if you already have one
  3. Fill out the online application form
  4. Upload your resume and required documents
  5. Submit your application
You'll receive a confirmation email once your application is submitted successfully.

Required documents typically include:
  • Updated resume/CV
  • Cover letter
  • Educational certificates
  • Professional certifications (if applicable)
  • Valid ID or passport copy
  • Recent passport-size photograph
Specific requirements may vary by position, so please check the job posting carefully.

The application process timeline varies depending on the position and number of applicants. Generally:
  • Initial application review: 1-2 weeks
  • First interview: Within 2-3 weeks of application
  • Additional interviews/assessments: 1-2 weeks
  • Final decision: Within 1-2 weeks of final interview
We strive to keep candidates informed throughout the process.

Benefits & Compensation

Berhan Bank offers a comprehensive benefits package including:
  • Competitive salary packages
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Life and disability insurance
  • Retirement benefits
  • Annual leave and sick leave
  • Performance bonuses
  • Staff loan facilities
  • Professional development support

Performance reviews are conducted:
  • Formally twice a year (mid-year and year-end)
  • Informal quarterly check-ins with supervisors
  • Additional reviews during probation period
Reviews include performance evaluation, goal setting, and career development discussions.
